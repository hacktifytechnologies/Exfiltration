## Fill 1
`tar` flag combination to create, compress with gzip, and specify output file:
**Answer:** `czf` (create, gzip compress, file)

## Fill 2
`zip` flag to password-protect an archive:
**Answer:** `-P <password>` (or `-e` for interactive password prompt)
